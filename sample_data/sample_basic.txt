Our company offers three main services: hair cutting, beard trimming, and hair coloring.

Hair cutting costs $20 and takes about 30 minutes. We are open Monday to Saturday from 9am to 7pm.

Beard trimming costs $10 and takes about 15 minutes. Walk-ins are welcome but appointments are preferred.

Hair coloring starts at $50 depending on hair length. It takes about 2 hours. Please book at least 2 days in advance.

To book an appointment call us at 555-1234 or visit our website. Our shop is located at 123 Main Street.

We do not open on Sundays. Holiday hours may vary. Gift cards are available at the front desk.
